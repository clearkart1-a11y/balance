How a Balance Score is measured

  • Ownership spread — is the company owned by few, or by many (co-ops, employees, small shareholders)?
  • Value return — how much revenue returns to workers, suppliers, and local communities?
  • Market share — does one player dominate, or is the market genuinely competitive?
  • Political influence — does economic power buy lobbying power?
