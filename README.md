# Balance ⚖️

**An awareness platform for world equilibrium.**

Balance is a concept-stage platform working toward one goal: political and economic balance in the world — *socialistic capitalism*. Markets that create wealth, guided by a conscience that shares it.

## The Three Balances

- **Political Balance** — can a common man stand as a representative without a political party? Restoring the individual's voice against two-party dualism
- **Economic Balance** — how many companies control a market, and who shares in the wealth they create? Concentration vs. distribution, made visible
- **Social Balance** — the real impact of social, cultural, and religious practices; living culture vs. empty ritualism

## The Idea

- **Awareness first** — make visible who holds power and where money flows
- **Balance Scores** — rate companies on whether they concentrate or distribute wealth
- **Conscious commerce** — products/services surfaced in service of the mission
- **Global equilibrium** — a yearly World Balance Index across nations

## This Repo

A static website (concept demo). No build step, no dependencies, all visuals are inline SVG.

- `index.html` — homepage with the three clickable balance cards
- `political.html` — party dualism vs. individual representation
- `economic.html` — company vs. alternative comparisons with demo Balance Scores
- `social.html` — living culture vs. empty ritualism
- `actions.html` — gamified action tracker (points, levels, badges, streaks, balance triangle) — stores data in the browser's localStorage only

## Run Locally

Just open `index.html` in a browser.
